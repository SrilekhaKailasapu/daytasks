About

The Hashtag Cafe Kphb is a F&B-Casual Dining outlet located in Kukatpally, Hyderabad. It offers a wide variety of delicious food and drinks in a cozy and relaxed atmosphere. The cafe has a modern and vibrant interior with comfortable seating arrangements. The menu includes a variety of North Indian, South Indian, Chinese and Continental dishes. The cafe also serves a variety of beverages like shakes, smoothies, mocktails and more. The cafe also offers a variety of desserts and snacks. The staff is friendly and attentive and the service is prompt. The Hashtag Cafe Kphb is the perfect place to enjoy a delicious meal with friends and family.
